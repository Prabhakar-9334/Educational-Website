Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know the right courses for me?

For selection of right courses, you have to first choose your domain then you may opt courses according to your choice.

How will I be assessed to any courses?

As of now, courses are limited. We will try to upload the courses of all subjests within stipulated time. For now you may visit our course section.

Do courses have any time limit?

No, Tutorials courses are self-paced, meaning you can take as much time as you need to complete a course.

What languages are courses offered in?

For now, we are offering courses in English language only.

How do I see what courses are available?

All Tutorials’s available courses can be accessed here: https://tutorials.org/courses . You can also access them by clicking on “Courses” in the top navigation bar.

Does Tutorials offer certificates?

No, Tutorials will not offer any certificate. It is a free platform you may learn from different channels provided in this page.

What types of courses does Tutorials offer?

Tutorials offers three levels of courses: basic, intermediate and advanced. video content will be availabe and you may download the slides.

Do I have to pay for Tutorials courses?

No, all Tutorials courses are free and open to anyone wishing to register.

Who can access Tutorials courses?

Tutorials courses are offered for free to anyone who registers with their email address.

How are courses structured?

Many courses consist of a combination of downloadable video lectures and presentation slides, as well as self-assessments to test your knowledge. Discussion forums are also available for users to share insight with experts in the field.
